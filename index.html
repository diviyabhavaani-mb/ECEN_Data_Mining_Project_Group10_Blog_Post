Abstract

This project studies handwritten Japanese character recognition on the Kuzushiji-MNIST (KMNIST) dataset. We compare logistic regression and multiple CNN architectures, achieving up to 97% accuracy with a tuned CNN.

Dataset Overview

The KMNIST dataset contains 70,000 grayscale images of Japanese characters across 10 classes.
